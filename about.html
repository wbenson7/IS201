About Me

Hobbies, Values, and Passions

I'm someone who loves staying active and getting outside as much as possible. Skiing is one of my favorite things in the world — there's just something about fresh snow, good views, and being in the mountains that never gets old. Whether it's skiing, surfing, or really anything outdoors, I'm always up for an adventure.

I’m a big believer in hard work and keeping myself busy. I like having goals to chase and things to look forward to. I love traveling and getting to experience new places, meet new people, and try things I’ve never done before. There’s just so much out there to see and learn.

School has also been a huge part of my life, and I’m super grateful for the chance to get an education. I love learning new things and figuring out how to apply them to real life. At the end of the day, I’m just someone who likes to stay moving, keep learning, and make the most out of every day.
